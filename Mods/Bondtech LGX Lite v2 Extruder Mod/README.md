# BOM:

**You'll need to print the Toolhead belt Connector and the x-carriage front files provided in the plated parts file.**


You also need the below hardware: 

| #  |   Item   |  ISO Number | 
| :---:  | :--- | :---|

| 11 | M3 nuts    | ISO 4032 
| 5 | M3x12 bolts | ISO 4762



# Assembly

Assembly is similar to standard Magpie toolhead, but you mount two the extruder to the toolhead belt connector with two  m3x12 bolts, and then mount the toolhead connector to the x-carriage front with three m3x12 bolts. 
As is typical, you'll need to embed hex nuts wherever a hexagonal hole is in on the printed parts.
The x-carriage front also has two places to slide a hex nut into at the top.
