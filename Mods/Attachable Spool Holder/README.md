## Summary
This is a spool holder for the Magpie, printable and attachable to the frame.

## Details
![Image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/380dbfb8-0e84-4507-970a-c7d0228ee63a)

![Image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e6f63d98-ca4c-404a-ae47-a910f970dbb3)

You install it by placing a hex nut into a one of the provided channels on the top frame on either the left or right side, and then line up the hole on of the spool holder with nut, and mate the spool holder to the frame with an m3x12 socket head screw (ISO 4762)
